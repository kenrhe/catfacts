ABOUT US

What is Facts Me Up?

Facts Me Up is a simple aplication that allows users to create lists of facts that range from any topics, from cats to scube diving. Other users can then subscribe to these facts depending n what they like and receive these facts on their phones.

Who are we?

We are a team of high schoolers from Bergen County Academies that love programming and kicking ass.

Why is this even a thing?

The idea for Fax Me Up originated from CatFacts, which had people forcefully subscribed to these facts about cats and unable to unsubscribe.
